About BioDB

The project was developed at Boston University as part of BF768 Biological Database Analysis, Spring 2025, under the guidance of Dr. Gary Benson.

Lost in the chaos of biological data? BioDB, built by Team 15 @ BU CDS, is designed to support the exploration of disease biology through structured, high-quality data. It integrates curated information from authoritative bioinformatics sources including UniProt, Gene Ontology, Reactome, KEGG, STRING, and Open Targets, enabling users to systematically query and analyze relationships among genes, proteins, pathways, and diseases.

Our Mission

Our goal is to make complex biological knowledge accessible and useful for researchers, students, and healthcare professionals. By grounding AI-driven analyses in structured and validated datasets, BioDB facilitates accurate, reproducible insights for applications in genomics, drug discovery, and systems biology.
